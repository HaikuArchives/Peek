Keyboard shortcuts & similar things

Hi.: A few of the important menu commands have short-cuts and you can find out what they are by looking in the Menu. However, there are others which are not easily visible because they either aren't "menu" material or because they affect the functioning of certain parts of Peek.
Here they are:
ESC If a slideshow is busy running, ESC will stop it.
  else, Peek will quit.

This is quite useful because if you double click on an image icon in Tracker and Peek loads, you can just press ESC and it's gone..

CTRL Holding down CTRL *BEFORE* you begin dragging an image or thumbnail will switch between the different drag & drop modes. IE, if you've set negotiated drag & drop as your default, holding ctrl will toggle simple drag & drop until you've released it.
Spacebar This will select the next image in the file list.
